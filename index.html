Synesthesia

Sasha vom Dorp / Vimeo
Synesthesia was created in collaboration with composer Eric Rosse. The video shows a real representation of how the sounds emanating from the Sound Illumintor are affecting the light. The higher color tones are created by tone sensitive LED lights that are coded with a mathematical equivalent to sound, creating a sound to light synesthesia.

Sound Mixing light - 34 ways

Sasha vom Dorp / Vimeo
Sasha Raphael vom Dorp is a multi-media artist who works with light and sound. In this work from his Sound Bending Light series, he illuminates sound waves using both natural sunlight, and tone sensitive LED's. All of the imagery in this work is induced in real-time by the sound frequencies of the music.
